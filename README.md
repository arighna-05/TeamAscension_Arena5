# 🌾 AgriLink — The AI Co-Pilot for Farmers

> **Sell Smart. Waste Less. Earn More.**

---

## 🚀 Overview

AgriLink is a **hyperlocal, AI-powered platform** designed to reduce agricultural waste and increase farmer income by connecting farmers with nearby buyers for surplus produce.

Unlike traditional marketplaces, AgriLink focuses on **urgent surplus management**, enabling farmers to sell excess crops quickly before spoilage.

---

## ❗ Problem Statement

A significant portion of harvested crops in India goes to waste due to:

- ❌ Lack of immediate buyers  
- ❌ Poor price discovery  
- ❌ Inefficient logistics  
- ❌ Limited access to technology  

> Farmers often incur losses despite having valuable produce.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

AgriLink acts as an **AI Co-Pilot** that helps farmers:

- 🌾 Identify surplus crops  
- 📍 Find nearby buyers instantly  
- 💰 Get fair price insights  
- 📱 Manage sales through a simple interface
